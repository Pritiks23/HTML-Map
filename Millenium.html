Vegan Guide Map of Los Angeles

This interactive map shows Vegan Restaurants near Millenium Dance Complex a popular location for dancers to train from all around the world. As professional dancers need to usually eat healthy, I wanted to showcase areas and routes to popular vegan restaurants near MDC. I provided various routes for walking and cycling to some places. The map targets both local dancers and tourists interested in finding healthy vegan resturants near the place where they spend a large amount of time daily. I chose Thunderforest's Landscape style so I can show terrain information and help in navigation. Additionally, the map includes functions such as address search and a locate control to help with interactive navigation.
